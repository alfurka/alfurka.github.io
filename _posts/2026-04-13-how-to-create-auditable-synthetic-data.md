I recently started a YouTube channel, and my main goal with it is **research translation**. It’s one thing to [publish a paper in a journal](/2025-12-16-synthetic-data-paper-published/), but it’s another entirely to get those methods out of the academic bubble and into the hands of data scientists and researchers who can actually use them.

In this video, I walk through the practical side of generating synthetic tabular data that balances high utility with robust privacy protection, specifically focusing on the `synloc` Python package.
