I presented *Specialist Incentives, AI Adoption, and Patient Welfare in Australia* at the MUCHE internal seminars in December 2025. This is joint work with `Jonas Fooken`, and the discussion was especially helpful for clarifying how to frame the paper conceptually.

This project is currently an opinion piece that tries to develop a political economy perspective on AI adoption in specialist care in Australia. At this stage, the focus is not on empirics but on building a coherent conceptual framework, and the seminar feedback was very useful for that.
