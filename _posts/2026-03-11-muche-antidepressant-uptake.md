I presented *The Impact of Antidepressant Uptake on Labor Productivity: Evidence from Australian Linked Administrative Data* at the MUCHE internal seminars on 11 March 2026. This is joint work with `Jonas Fooken` and `Henry Cutler`, and it was a helpful opportunity to share updated results and receive detailed feedback before the next conference presentation.

Our paper uses Australian linked administrative data to study how antidepressant uptake affects labour productivity. The seminar discussion was very useful for refining the empirical presentation and sharpening the interpretation of the main findings.
